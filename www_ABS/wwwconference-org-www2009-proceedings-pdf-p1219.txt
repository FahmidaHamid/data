in this paper, we study a novel problem of staring people discovery from social networks, which is concerned with  nding people who are not only authoritative but also sociable in the social network. we formalize this problem as an optimization programming problem. taking the coauthor network as a case study, we de ne three objective functions and propose two methods to combine these objective functions. a genetic algorithm based method is further presented to solve this problem. experimental results show that the proposed solution can e ectively  nd the staring people from social networks.
